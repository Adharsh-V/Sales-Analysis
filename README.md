# Sales-Analysis
• Designed and delivered a dynamic Sales Analytics Dashboard in Power BI with advanced 
time intelligence and parameter-driven filtering (last N days/months). 
• Enabled performance tracking across sales representatives, active customers, and product 
lines (GTI, PM, IAF), incorporating KPI metrics such as sales amount, sales count, and 
discount analysis. 
• Built historical item-level trend reporting to support strategic, data-driven business 
decisions.
